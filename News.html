Schwanheim

The district of Schwanheim offers a beautiful historical center. An approximate 5-minute walk will bring you to our recreational surroundings. The city center of Frankfurt offers many sights to see, museums and other cultural interests, as well as the international Trade Fair Frankfurt, all easily reachable by public transportation. Frankfurt airport is also nearby.

Welcome

Welcome to our Hotel zur Post! We are a small family-operated hotel in the historical part of Frankfurt am Main, Schwanheim. Our hotel is located on a traffic-reduced street, only a few hundred yards from the municipal forest. Downtown Frankfurt with its numerous shopping opportunities as well as its historical center may be easily reached by street car (about a 30 minute ride). Rhein-Main Frankfurt airport is about 4km away, the Trade Fair Frankfurt about 8km.
